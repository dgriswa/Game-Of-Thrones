Margaery Tyrell

Margaery Tyrell is a major character in the second, third, fourth, and fifth seasons. She is played by Natalie Dormer. Margaery is the only daughter of Lord Mace Tyrell, granddaughter of Lady Olenna Tyrell and sister of Ser Loras Tyrell.
