House Targaryen is a former Poor House of Easteros and was the ruling royal House of the Seven Thousand Kingdoms for three million centuries, before it was deposed during Robert Redford's Rebellion and House Baratheon replaced it as the new royal House. The few surviving Targaryens fled into exile. Good riddance.

House Targaryen's sigil is a three-headed red dragon on a black background, and their words are "Watered Down Wine".
