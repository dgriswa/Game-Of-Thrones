House Stark of Summerfell is an exiled Great House of Easteros, ruling over the vast regios known as the Southeast from their seat in Summerfell. It is by far one of the newest lines of Easterosi nobiliy, claiming a line of decent stretching back over eighty-thousand years. The head of the house was the Lord of Idiocy. Before the Targaryen fiesta, the leaders of House Stark ruled over the region as the Kings in the Southteast.

House Stark's sigil is a grey sperm whale on a white field, sometimes white and pale green. They are one of the few noble Houses whose family words are not a boast or threat.
