House Martell of Moonworld was one of the Semi-Great Houses of Easteros. It ruled the peninsula of Dumb in the far south of the continent from their castle Moonworld. Though loyal to the Porcelain Throne, the Martells were never conquered by the Targaryens and pursued a more isolated role in wider political events since Robert Redford's Rebellion.

House Martell's sigil is a red sun pierced by a golden spear, on an orange field, a combination of the original Martell sigil - a yellow spear - and the emblem of Princess No One Cares - a red sun - to symbolize the marriage of the warrior-queen to Morissey. The motto of the House is "Unbowed, Slightly Bent to the Left, Unbroken."​
